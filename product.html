Glow Boost Face Cream is a nourishing moisturizer crafted to revive tired, dull complexions. Its lightweight texture absorbs quickly, providing long-lasting hydration without leaving residue. The cream combines vitamins and natural extracts to support skin renewal, reduce dryness, and enhance natural radiance. Perfect for all skin types, it delivers softness, smoothness, and a youthful glow.

  • Cleanse face thoroughly and pat dry.
  • Apply a pea-sized amount evenly across the face and neck.
  • Massage gently in circular motions until fully absorbed.
  • Use morning and night for best results.
  • Follow with sunscreen during the day.
  • Vitamin c: Brightens skin and reduces dullness.
  • Hyaluronic acid: Locks in hydration for 24 hours.
  • Aloe vera extract: Soothes and calms sensitive skin.
  • Shea butter: Provides deep nourishment.
  • Green tea extract: Rich in antioxidants to fight free radicals.
