Shipping policy

Delivery

We strive to ensure prompt and reliable delivery of your orders. Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days, and shipping times vary based on your location. Standard shipping usually takes 5-7 business days, while expedited options are available for faster delivery. You will receive a tracking number once your order has shipped so you can monitor its progress. Please note that delivery times may be affected by factors such as weather conditions or high order volumes. If you have any questions or need assistance, contact our support team.

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days.

Expedited shipping options are available.

Track your order with the provided tracking number.

Freight

Shipping costs vary based on your location and the weight of your order.

We offer a range of shipping options to meet your needs, including standard, expedited, and overnight services. Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight, dimensions, and destination of your order. You will see the total shipping charge at checkout before you complete your purchase. We strive to provide the most cost-effective shipping methods while ensuring timely delivery. During checkout, you can choose the shipping option that best suits your needs. For large or heavy items, additional shipping fees may apply.

Shipping charges are shown at checkout before completing your purchase.

Choose from standard, expedited, or overnight shipping options.

Dispatch

Orders are dispatched within 1-2 business days after being placed. We carefully prepare and package each order to ensure it arrives in perfect condition. Once your order is on its way, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. This allows you to monitor the progress of your shipment. Please allow additional time during peak seasons or for custom orders. If there are any issues or delays with your shipment, our customer service team is here to help. For any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to reach out.

Orders are sent out within 1-2 business days.

Receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Allow extra time during busy periods.

Contact customer service for shipping issues.

Postage

We cover the cost of standard postage for all domestic orders.

For international shipments, postage costs are calculated based on the destination, weight, and dimensions of your package. You will be notified of the postage fees during checkout, and they will be added to your order total. We use reliable carriers to ensure your package reaches you safely and in a timely manner. Please be aware that customs fees or import taxes may apply, and these are the responsibility of the recipient. If you have any questions about international postage or need assistance, please contact our support team for help.

International postage fees are calculated during checkout.

Customs fees or import taxes are the recipient's responsibility.
